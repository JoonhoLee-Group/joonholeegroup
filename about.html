Hello

I completed my PhD dissertation study, "Postmodern Electronic Structure Theory", under the guidance of Prof. Martin Head-Gordon at University of California, Berkeley in 2019. I am finishing my postdoc training with Prof. David Reichman at Columbia University in 2023. Since 2021, I have been a visiting faculty researcher with the quantum algorithms team at Google Quantum AI. More information about me is available in my personal website.

I am an electronic structure theorist by training and I have broad research interests in chemistry, materials science, and condensed matter physics.

I am joining the CCB faculty at Harvard University as an Assistant Professor in July 2023.
